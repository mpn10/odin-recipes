White Rice

Description

Cooking rice is probably the easiest thing in the world. And it is made even easier with this recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 of cup long grain white rice
  • 2 cups of water

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice first before cooking to remove the starch.
  2. Pour 1 cup of rice and then 2 cups of water into the pot. Then turn on the heat with the lid opened.
  3. After the water starts boiling, cover the lid and reduce the heat.
  4. Fluff the rice after cooking, using a fork.
