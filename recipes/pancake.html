Pancake Recipe

Description

This simple recipe makes pancakes that are light, fluffy and tasty. You just need a couple of basic ingredients that you most likely have in your kitchen at this moment.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of floor
  • 2 tablespons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Add the dry ingredients in a large bowl and whisk to combine.
  2. Add the wet ingredients into the dry mixture and whisk until the batter is almost smooth.
  3. After letting the batter rest for 10 minutes, heat a large cast iron.
  4. Add a teaspoon of vegetable oil to the pan and swirl. Add 2 tablespoons of the batter to the batter. Cook 3 at a time, making sure to cook each side for 3 minutes.
