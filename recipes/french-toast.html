French Toast

Description

Start your day off with a full stomach with this recipe of french toast.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2cups of milk
  • 2 teaspoons of cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cardamom
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • 8 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Using a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, cinnamon, salt and cardamom
  2. Dip the slices of bread into the mixture, putting the soaked bread slices on a large plate after.
  3. Using a non-sticky skillet, cook the soaked bread slices until they're golden brown. Do this for 2 minutes on each side of the bread.
